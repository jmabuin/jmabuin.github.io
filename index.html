Welcome to my personal page

Thanks for your visit! My name is José Manuel Abuín Mosquera. I am a computer engineer and researcher at the University of Santiago de Compostela in the Centro de Investigación en Tecnoloxías da Información (CiTIUS).

Previously I worked at the Applied Mathematics Department, also at this University, as researcher and programmer, and at the Galicia Supercomputing Centre, as computer engineer, participating in european projects.

My main interests are focused on High Performance Computing, Big Data technologies, parallel computing, scientific simulation, irregular and sparse matrix problems and open source software.

I am a software developer and a Linux and Open Source Software fan. You can find more information about in this page.
