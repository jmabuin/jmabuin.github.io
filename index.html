Welcome to my personal page

Thanks for your visit! My name is José Manuel Abuín Mosquera. I am a computer scientist, researcher and Phd. student at the University of Santiago de Compostela in the Centro de Investigación en Tecnoloxías da Información (CiTIUS).

Previously I worked at the Applied Mathematics Department, also at this University, as researcher and programmer, and at the Galicia Supercomputing Centre, as computer engineer, participating in european projects.

My main research interests are focused on

  • High Performance Computing.
  • Big Data technologies.
  • Parallel computing.
  • Bioinformatics and genomics
  • Optimization of scientific modelling software
  • Irregular and sparse matrix problems.
